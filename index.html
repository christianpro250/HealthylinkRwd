What we offer

Everything your sector needs
to stay healthy.

📋

Structured Reporting

Submit validated health incident reports with rich location data, severity levels, and photo evidence. Forms built with Bootstrap and client-side validation prevent bad data at entry.

📊

Real-Time Dashboard

Visualise trends across sectors and districts. Identify outbreak patterns, track follow-ups, and export reports for district health officers — all from a single interface.

📡

Social Feed Integration

Stay informed via curated health updates from official Twitter/X and Facebook pages. Community voices and official alerts — aggregated in one feed.

🗺️

District Coverage

Built to scale from one pilot sector to all 30 districts of Rwanda. The data model is district-agnostic so any community can onboard without code changes.

🔔

Instant Alerts

Health workers receive SMS and email notifications when a new high-severity report is filed in their sector. Response times reduced from days to hours.

🔒

Secure & Private

All reports are encrypted in transit. Reporter identities are protected by default. Administrators can access anonymised data while preserving community trust.
